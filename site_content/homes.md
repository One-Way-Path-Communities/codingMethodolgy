# HOMES

## Expanding Housing Where It Is Needed Most

Ontario continues to face a significant housing supply shortfall, with demand outpacing new housing starts in many regions. Expanding attainable, family-friendly housing is essential to restoring balance and supporting long-term affordability.

We develop thoughtfully designed homes that respond directly to this need.

---

## Designed for Real Living

Our projects include a range of suite types — including three-bedroom, family-oriented layouts — to serve diverse households.

Thoughtful floorplans, durable materials, and efficient systems create homes that are comfortable, practical, and built to last.

---

## Typical Project Mix

- **140 Suites per Project**  
- **24% Multi-tenant Units**  
- **1,000+ Sq. Ft. Townhouses**

A balanced mix of housing types supports both affordability and long-term community stability.

---

## Why Supply Matters

Increasing housing supply:

- Reduces pressure on rents and prices  
- Improves availability of family-sized units  
- Supports labour mobility  
- Strengthens local economies  

Housing is foundational infrastructure. Delivering more well-built homes is essential to community resilience.
