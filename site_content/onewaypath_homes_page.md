# HOMES

## Addressing a Critical Need for Housing in Ontario

Ontario faces a significant housing supply challenge, with demand outpacing new housing starts in many regions. Expanding attainable, family-friendly housing is essential to restoring balance and long-term affordability.

We deliver thoughtfully designed homes across a range of sizes — including three-bedroom suites — so households can stay and thrive in the communities they love.
