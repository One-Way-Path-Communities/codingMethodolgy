# SENIORS

## Aging in Place, Within the Community

Many seniors want to remain close to family, services, and familiar surroundings, but do not want to live in a retirement home and lack appropriate rental options to downsize.

We develop purpose-built rental housing that enables this transition — offering more manageable homes within the communities residents already know.

Our approach is simple: well-designed homes, with access to services when needed.

---

## Independent Living, With Flexibility

Residents live independently in thoughtfully designed suites, with the ability to access additional support services over time as needs evolve.

- Private, self-contained rental homes
- Access to on-demand services
- Curated community connections
- Flexibility to scale support over time

---

## Access to Services and Community Life

Residents benefit from access to a range of in-home services and community-based programs that support daily living, health, and social connection.

This includes support such as transportation, housekeeping, personal care, wellness services, and opportunities to participate in shared activities and community life.

Services are flexible and can be tailored to individual preferences.
